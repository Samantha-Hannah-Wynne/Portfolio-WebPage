About DBS

Dublin Business School (DBS) offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the fields of computing, business, and other disciplines. It is known for its focus on practical learning, with a strong emphasis on industry relevance.

My Program

I am currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Computing (Data Analytics & Big Data) at DBS. The program focuses on data analysis, cloud computing, and software development, providing a solid foundation in these essential areas of computing.

Why I Chose DBS

I chose DBS because of its strong reputation in providing hands-on experience, the emphasis on small class sizes, and the opportunities to network with industry professionals. The curriculum aligns perfectly with my career aspirations in data analytics and big data.

  • Introduction to Cloud Computing
